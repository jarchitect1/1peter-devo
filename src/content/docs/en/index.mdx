<p class="landing-lead opener">A devotional series through Peter's first letter, prepared for an evangelical assembly with a brethren background. The aim is not exegesis or sermon prep, but **devotional depth** — feeding the congregation with the actual text of Scripture, one passage at a time.</p>

The through-line is the **priesthood of all believers** (1 Pet 2:9): who we are in Christ shapes how we live in the world. Lessons are short, self-contained, and meant to be returned to.

<Aside title="Where to start" type="note">
New to the series? Begin with [Lesson 1, an overview of the whole letter](/en/0001-a-living-hope-for-sojourners/), then keep the [at-a-glance map](/en/1-peter-at-a-glance/) open as you walk through, passage by passage.
</Aside>
